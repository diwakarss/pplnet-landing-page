The People Net

Empowering individuals through Blockchain. A digital society where individuals exercise full control over their data and financial transactions, and democratic principles flourish in an autonomous, decentralized environment. To bring this vision to life, we are developing a technology ecosystem founded on principles of Acentric Autonomous Organisation (AAO), ZK Rollups, and Solid PODs model for data privacy.

Ecosystem

Acentric Autonomous Organisations (AAO)

At the heart of PPL lies the AAO framework, a unique system where every user is an autonomous entity, forming the basis for decentralized governance. AAOs ensure that power isn't centralized but distributed, allowing individuals to manage their digital and financial interactions with full autonomy and security.

zkRollup-Based Scalability

Integrating zkRollup technology, PPL offers a revolutionary approach to blockchain scalability and security. This structure ensures rapid, low-cost transactions without compromising on the decentralized ethos, making PPL a frontrunner in next-gen blockchain solutions.

Solid PODs Data Sovereignty

Our commitment to user privacy is unwavering. With the Solid PODs model, PPL ensures that users have unparalleled control over their data. Every piece of information is securely stored, managed, and shared based on the user's consent, revolutionizing the way we think about data in the digital age.

Democratic Digital Governance

Drawing from the principles of direct democracy and the Network State, PPL champions a governance model where every voice counts. It promotes an ecosystem where decisions are made collectively, ensuring transparency, fairness, and equal representation in shaping the future of the digital realm.
